Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Patrick HAGOT ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Patrick HAGOT mobile application (the "App").

The App is designed to allow clients to securely access photos and videos captured by the photographer. Access is provided via a username and password shared directly with the client.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

To access the App, users are provided with login credentials. We collect and store:

  • Username
  • Encrypted password

These credentials are used solely for authentication and secure access to private media content.

1.2 Media Content

The App provides access to photos and videos captured by the photographer. These files:

  • Are uploaded by the photographer or authorized administrators
  • Are accessible only to authenticated users
  • Are not publicly visible

1.3 Device & Usage Information

The App may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and performance data

This data is used only to improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including secure authentication and restricted access to private media.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
